Easy 1 hour
Creme Brûlée!
For 7-8

This super simple Creme Brûlée recipe is not only tasty, but it is sure to impress guests! The best part is how little ingredients it requires. Be sure to have a blow torch to melt the sugar on top!

Ingredients

  • 1 Quart Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Vanilla extract
  • 6 Egg Yolks
  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • Love

See Ingredients

Boil

Combine 1 quart heavy whipping cream in a pot with vanilla extract and bring to a boil. once it reaches a boil remove from heat and cool for 15 minutes.

Whisk

Whisk together 6 egg yolks and 1/2 cup sugar until light and frothy

Combine

Slowly add the cream to the eggs and sugar until combined

Bake

Pour into ramekins and bake in a water bath at 325F for 40-45 minutes

Cool

Cool for at least 30 minutes. When ready to eat, take it out and let it come to room temp. and then add sugar to the top and brûlée!
